Why did you make this?

Justify is an imperfect attempt to help you make a conscious choice about the artists you support.

In an era of very loud, very public deplatforming campaigns and cancel culture, we wanted to create a simple, more nuanced way for individuals to do the right thing — something we do by helping you remove the problematic performers you forgot you follow, or songs you added to playlists in more innocent days.

***
Which artists does this apply to, and why?

Actually deciding who to include on this list was challenging.

Since Justify is about personally opting-out of an artist (vs. deplatforming), we've included people with credible allegations against them, even if they've never been found guilty.

Ultimately this is an arbitrary list based on our personal feelings. Don't click the button blindly — look at this list and draw your own boundries.
