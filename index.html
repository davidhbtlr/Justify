Justify is an attempt to help you make a conscious choice about the artists you support.

Amid the loud, public deplatforming campaigns and cancel culture, Justify is a simple, quieter way to do the right thing — namely by ensuring songs in old playlists or artists you followed years ago aren't helping some dirtbag's Spotify ranking.

Have questions, suggestions, or just want to talk? E-mail us! (We'll get back to you as quickly as we can.)
