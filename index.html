Why DOES THIS Exist?

Justify is an imperfect attempt to help you make a conscious choice about the artists you support.

In an era of very loud, very public deplatforming campaigns and cancel culture, Justify is a simple, more nuanced way for individuals to do the right thing — namely by ensuring artists you forgot you followed or songs in old playlists aren't helping some dirtbag's Spotify ranking.

Which artists does this apply to, and why?

allegations against them, even if they've never been found guilty. Ultimately this is an arbitrary list based on our personal feelings.

Don't click the button blindly — take a minute to look at these artists and make up your own mind. That's the real point of this tool.
